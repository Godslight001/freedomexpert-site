Teaching Financial Freedom: 3-Week Financial Stewardship Program

This is not your typical financial class—it's a transformative experience focused on financial stewardship and development. Over three weeks, you’ll gain not only core principles but also practical tools and apps to streamline your financial journey.

Think of it as a conversation with friends who uplift, inspire, and help reignite the dreams God has placed in your heart—without guilt or shame. Rooted in Biblical wisdom, this program will equip you with actionable strategies to manage your finances in alignment with God's purpose, empowering you to live a life of true financial freedom—spiritually, financially, and scripturally.

Financial Stewardship Book Study: 4-Week Program

Dive deep into a highly recommended book on financial stewardship in this engaging study. Participants will read and analyze each chapter, using workbook-style learning to break down key principles and create actionable steps to address specific financial challenges.

Rooted in biblical wisdom, whatever literature is studied will serve as a clear guide to help you earn more, manage your finances with ease, and embrace the joy of giving. Grounded in grace and filled with hope, it inspires practical, positive changes in your financial journey.

This 4-week program is ideal for your church, education centers, or exclusive groups, offering valuable financial stewardship and development principles that are both scripturally sound and financially empowering.
